What's New

  • Hillside 'virtual' and 'in-person'

    Hillside planned to make a way for people to show up to school in-person, and a schedule for people at home. So they did. You can choose from in-person, (live school taught in-person) to virtual (work from home). This way, there is an organized way for people who want to show up to the schoo lto do that, and for virtual people to do that too.

  • New 'Canvas' virtual website

    At Hillside you can choose virtual or in-person, but either way they are using a site called 'Canvas', a website used for tracking and grading homework and tests. For virtual kids, this is what they use every day. To going to the reading course, then to the math course, and so on every day.
